How It Helps People

Fatter Matter Calorie Tracker helps people understand what they are eating by showing clear information about scanned food items.

It makes nutrition details easier to find, especially for users who may not want to read long labels or search online.

This can support healthier choices by giving people quick access to calorie counts and basic nutrition facts.

The project also helps users learn more about their food in a simple and interactive way.

Overall, it provides a faster and more convenient way to check important information about everyday items.

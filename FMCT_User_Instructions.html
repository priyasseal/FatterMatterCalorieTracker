User Instructions

1. Turn on the Fatter Matter Calorie Tracker device.

2. Hold a food item so the barcode is facing the scanner.

3. Slowly move the barcode across the scanner until it beeps or reads the code.

4. Look at the screen to see the item name and basic nutrition information.

5. Repeat the process for any other items you want to check.

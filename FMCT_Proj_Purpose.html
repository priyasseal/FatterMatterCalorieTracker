Project Purpose

Fatter Matter was created to help people quickly learn important information about the food items they scan.

When a barcode is read, the device shows details like the product name and basic nutrition facts.

This makes it easier for users to understand what they are eating without having to search for the information themselves.

The project also demonstrates how simple technology can be used to solve everyday problems.

Overall, Fatter Matter aims to make food information faster, clearer, and more accessible for everyone.
